About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer with a Master's degree in Data Science from AIMS Rwanda and a Bachelor's degree in Statistics. With a strong foundation in statistical analysis and machine learning, I specialize in developing end-to-end data solutions that drive business value.
