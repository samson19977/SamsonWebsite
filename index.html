About Me

I'm a Data Scientist and ML Engineer based in Kigali, Rwanda, currently working as Data Manager & Researcher at the AIMS Research and Innovation Centre. I hold an MSc in Mathematical Sciences (Data Science) from AIMS Rwanda and a BSc in Mathematics with Statistics from the University of Rwanda.

My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, geospatial analytics, and real-world impact — from building early warning systems for air pollution to agentic AI tools for Rwandan farmers. I've presented research at international conferences including MLOA 2025 and SOSCHI 2025, and participated in advanced training programs across Africa and Europe.
